Vivin has joined the group of Prof. Dr. Peter Zaspel at the Jacobs University, Bremen to pursue his Ph.D. research in machine learning methods for quantum chemistry. 

![](/assets/media/2022/bremen.jpeg)

With an interdiscplinary background, a masters in economics and a bachelor's in physics with honours, Vivin is looking forward to gaining further expertise in machine learning methods specific to quantum chemical systems. His [master's thesis](/publications/Vinod_masters_dissertation) focussed on machine learning methods for financial markets.
